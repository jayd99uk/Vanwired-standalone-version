Privacy Policy for VanWired

Last Updated: October 8, 2025

Introduction

VanWired ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mobile application that provides electrical system calculators and checklists for campervan and motorhome conversions. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your information.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

All data you enter into VanWired is stored locally on your device only. This includes:

  • Calculator input values and results
  • Custom checklist categories
  • Checklist item completion status
  • App preferences and settings

How We Use Your Information

All data is stored locally using your device's browser storage (LocalStorage) and is used solely to:

  • Save your calculations for future reference
  • Maintain your checklist progress
  • Remember your app preferences

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us or any third parties.

Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device using browser LocalStorage
  • No data is transmitted over the internet
  • No account creation or login is required
  • Clearing your browser data or app cache will delete all saved information
  • Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data

Third-Party Services

VanWired does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

VanWired does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app is designed for adults performing electrical system installations.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Data Retention

All data is retained locally on your device until you:

  • Clear your browser/app data
  • Uninstall the application
  • Manually delete checklist categories or reset calculator values

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • You can view all stored data within the app
  • You can delete checklist categories at any time
  • You can clear all data by clearing browser/app storage
  • You can uninstall the app to remove all data
